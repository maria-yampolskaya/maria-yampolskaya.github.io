## Methods for Dynamics across Living Systems

**Deadline for registration and abstract submission: April 5, 2026**

Date: April 14, 2026, 8:30 - 17:30

Location: Room 6214/6254 and Salon ​Maurice L'Abbé, Pavillon André-Aisenstadt, Université de Montréal

This workshop aims to foster interdisciplinary exchange of quantitative tools for studying dynamical systems in biology. Disparate fields often develop specialized formalisms and methods; this workshop seeks to encourage scientists, especially trainees, to cross-pollinate ideas and frameworks, towards the goal of finding universal mathematical principles underlying biology. We’d like to especially encourage trainees to participate, both in giving talks and having discussions with their peers from other fields. These talks don't have to present polished results; works in progress are more than welcome. Experimentalists, theorists, and computational researchers are all invited. 

If you have any questions or comments, feel free to contact one of the organizers:
